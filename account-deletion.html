Account and Data Deletion

Effective Date: May 16, 2026

This page explains how users of Dream Way mobile games and services can request deletion of an account and associated personal data.

How to Request Deletion

To request account or data deletion, email us at:

dreamway.gamestudio@gmail.com

Please include the following information so we can identify your data:

  • The game name you used
  • The email address, player ID, or account ID connected to the game, if applicable
  • A short message saying that you want your account and data deleted

What Will Be Deleted

After verifying the request, we will delete personal data associated with your account where applicable, including profile or account information and app usage data connected to your account.

Game progress, saved preferences, and account-linked gameplay data may also be deleted when they are associated with the account or identifier provided in your request.

Data We May Need to Keep

Some limited records may be retained only when required for legal, security, fraud-prevention, accounting, or platform compliance reasons.

Processing Time

We aim to process deletion requests within 7 days after receiving enough information to identify the account or data.
